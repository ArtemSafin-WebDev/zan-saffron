At Zan Saffron we bring you a borderless experience by travelling to the best farms across various regions to select the most exquisite Saffron. We are serious about our hands-on approach to sourcing the best threads, which means you are consuming only the finest quality Persian Saffron.

Saffron is one of the most delicate and rare spices in the world. Enjoyed for both it’s vibrant color and unmistakable aroma it can be prepared and used in several ways, most commonly by adding it to food and drinks.

Saffron is technically the red threads found within the flower. This flower grows in specific temperatures and must be treated delicately to produce high quality saffron.

Saffron is commonly known as one of the most expensive spice in the world, but our vision is to bring the highest quality saffron to you.
